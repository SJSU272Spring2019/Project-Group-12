  How do I use the Break-In Monitor?


1. Query A Parking Spot

Input a desired Day of The Week, Time of Day, and Geographic Coordinates.

2. Calculate the Risk

Our Machine Learning Model accepts the parameters provided, and runs them in the backend script.

3. View the Map

With our calculated probability, you can see all queried parking locations on a user-friendly Google Map.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do we get our data?

Our data sets are taken from the Santa Clara County Sheriff.

How accurate is the machine learning model?

Currently, the model is only 70% accurate. This may affect percentage outcome values.

What do the color indicators mean for nodes?

Green: low risk. Yellow: slight risk. Orange: medium risk. Red: high risk.

Is this app viewable on mobile?

Yes, the Break-In Monitor is supported on mobile devices including tablets and smartphones.
